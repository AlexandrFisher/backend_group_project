Our Mission

To serve those who have served is an honor.


YOU ARE FAMILY. Our brothers, sisters, fathers, mothers, sons and daughters in arms. We understand better than most what it means to serve for country, faith, loves ones, and for a better tomorrow because we served for those reasons too. We know the highs and lows that come with military service. The disconnect and difficulty that occurs when the world around you, your inner thoughts and personal struggles lead to doubt and despair. WE ARE HERE to help you through these times of challenge. We are the keepers of our family and YOU ARE FAMILY.



If you are dealing with difficult times and need to chat anonymously or are willing to lend your time to those in their moment of need, YOU HAVE COME TO THE RIGHT PLACE. Welcome to Block-22 where we work to change the meaning and the very idea of '22'.
